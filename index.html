About Me

As a Senior DevOps Engineer, I bring a strong track record of building and maintaining secure, efficient infrastructure. My experience spans optimizing CI/CD pipelines, implementing GitOps workflows, and integrating DevSecOps principles to enhance security and reliability across complex, multi-cloud environments.

Throughout my career at Capgemini, VOIS – Vodafone Intelligent Solutions, and Etisalat by e&, I have led projects to streamline processes, automate infrastructure at scale, and enhance team productivity using technologies like Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins, GitHub Actions, and ArgoCD. My certifications — including the CNCF Kubestronaut designation and credentials in AWS, Azure, and Red Hat — validate my deep understanding of modern infrastructure and deployment environments.

My academic background includes a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Statistics from Helwan University, where I graduated with a Very Good cumulative grade and ranked third in my class. I also completed a 9-month diploma in System and DevOps Administration at the Information Technology Institute (ITI), further refining my technical foundation in the IT domain.

I thrive in challenging, fast-paced settings and enjoy leveraging innovative technologies to drive impactful, production-grade projects. My vision is infrastructure that works invisibly — where platforms are self-healing, security is baked in from day one, and engineering teams are free to focus entirely on building great products.

I look forward to connecting with professionals and exploring opportunities where I can contribute my expertise and passion for technology.
